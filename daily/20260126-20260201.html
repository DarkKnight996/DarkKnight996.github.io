    • authors: Evangelos Georganas, Alexander Heinecke, Pradeep Dubey
    • institution: Intel Corporation

  • [arXiv260126] Artifact for Service-Level Energy Modeling and Experimentation for Cloud-Native Microservices

    • authors: Julian Legler
    • institution: Technische Universität Berlin
    • link: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16635
    • Simple LLM Summary: The paper presents GOXN, an experimentation engine for measuring the energy consumption of Kubernetes-based microservices, using an additive model to derive service-level energy from container-level metrics.     • authors: Jun Doi, Tomonori Shirakawa, Yukio Kawashima, Seiji Yunoki, Hiroshi Horii
    • institution: IBM Quantum, IBM Research - Tokyo, RIKEN, Center for Computational Science
    • link: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16637
    • Simple LLM Summary: This paper presents a GPU-accelerated implementation of Selected Basis Diagonalization (SBD) for Sample-based Quantum Diagonalization (SQD) algorithms using the Thrust library.
